BEST PRACTICES FOR SETTING UP GOOGLE ADS SEARCH CAMPAIGNS

Campaign Structure Best Practices

  • Campaigns targeting top level products or services
  • Ad Groups that only have 1 x Keyword theme in each ad group
  • Each Ad Group having targeting Ad Copies that sent users to the most relevant product or service page
