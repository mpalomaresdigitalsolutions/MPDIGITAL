Performance Max Campaign Guide

Best Practices for Setting Up Performance Max Campaigns

1. Landing Page Selection

Pick your product landing pages carefully. Ensure page titles have a single keyword theme without including other keywords that Google might target.

2. Asset Groups Strategy

Add multiple Asset Groups that target product groups (NOT audiences or keyword themes). Each Asset group should:

  • Target one product category
  • Include Audience Signals targeting specific product-related keywords
  • Target specific Audiences & Demographics likely to convert

3. Ad Copy Optimization

Create compelling headlines and descriptions featuring:

  • Keyword Focus
  • Emotional Triggers (FOMO)
  • Clear Call to Action

4. Conversion Actions

Maintain only 1-2 active conversion actions, preferably focusing on Transactions over soft conversions.
